Els Incansables

Social Design

Hemophilia is an illness of genetic origin that prevents the blood from clotting correctly, which means that the persons with hemophilia bleed more than normal. The pathology can be slight, moderate or grave, depending on the amount of coagulation factor in the blood. Hemophihlia is generally diagnosed during lactation or infancy. The tools needed to explain the situation to kids have to be distinct from those used for adults. “Els Incansables” has been developed to improve the experience of the infants that suffer hemophilia. The project consists of the creation of a didactic game from a few pieces, which correspond to the parts of the organism that intervene in the illness and create some characters to interpret it.
